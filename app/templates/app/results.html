Pnemonia Symptoms
Fever | Chills | Breathlessness | Nausea | Sweating

Pnemonia Treatment
To treat the infection while at home, we advise you to consume plenty of warm fluids to bring up phlegm (mucus), soak the body in lukewarm water, do a salt water gargle, and most importantly rest for at least 8 hours.
